How we are working

Algorithm for working with us for convenience and understanding of the project

Idea

A client comes to us with an idea. This could be an image/sketch or other reference. And we think about how to implement it, make technical drawings and offer solutions on materials.

Technical task

Together with the client, we formulate the correct technical specification, which includes visualization of the product, a sketch drawing with dimensions, information on materials and finishing, project implementation period and other mandatory items.

Commercial offer

We preferably use natural materials. But we can optimize any estimate by simplifying the materials or design. We will be able to adapt to the client’s budget and agree on a commercial proposal.

Preparing a working draft

We will create working documentation and drawings. This is the foundation of quality production. At this stage, we approve with the client all dimensions, materials, drawings and begin production.

Production and installation

Production takes from 15 working days, depending on complexity and volume. We also do the installation ourselves. After all, we will do it quickly and accurately.
